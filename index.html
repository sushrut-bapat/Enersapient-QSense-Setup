Welcome to EnerSapient™ | QSense Setup

If you are setting up the device for the first time, you will have to bring the device over to the local Wi-Fi.

We will do this here along with complete device setup.

Please complete the power connections as per the Manual. Once powered the device will become an access point (AP).

Please start by connecting your laptop or cellphone to the AP named ENERSAPIENT-QSENSE.

Once connected, click the "Begin" button below to start the setup.

If your device is already on the local Wi-Fi, click "Go to Setup Portal" instead.
